# The Best Brief — 2026 Q1

> *Best Bank's internal newsletter. Published quarterly by Marketing & Comms (CMO James Whitfield).*

---

## 🪪 Headline: Best Credit is here

This quarter we launched **[Best Credit](./service-best-credit.md)** — the bank's first
credit product line and the biggest product event since [Best Savings in 2013](./company-history.md).
It includes the **Best Credit Card** (no annual fee, 2% back) and the **Best Line of Credit**.

> "Fifteen years in, we finally offer credit — and we're doing it the Best Bank way:
> conservative limits, transparent terms, no gotchas." — **Lisa Tran**, CPO

### How we're rolling it out

- **Existing deposit customers first.** Lowest acquisition cost and best risk profile,
  since we already see their cash flow. CMO **James Whitfield** is leading go-to-market.
- **In-house underwriting.** Credit Risk (Nadia Fields) scores applications using
  **Sentinel** signals plus deposit history from **Reservoir**.
- **Fraud-ready from day one.** Every card swipe is scored in real time by Sentinel;
  Card Ops handles disputes out of Atlanta.

## 🔧 Behind the scenes: data work

To support cards, the [Conduit](./data-pipeline-conduit.md) roadmap now includes new
data domains for card transactions, balances, and disputes (landing 2026 H2). Raj
Patel's team is partnering with Marcus Bell's Lending & Cards group to get the
**Lighthouse** adoption and loss-rate dashboards ready.

---

## 🎯 What success looks like

Per the [Financial Projections](./financial-projections.md):

- **~12%** of checking customers adopt Best Credit within 18 months.
- Best Credit balances reach **~$310M** by year-end 2026.
- Credit losses held near **2.8%** of balances; net fraud losses trending toward **<10bps**.
