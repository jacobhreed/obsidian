## Definition

Best Bank's culture is organized around three principles established by Eleanor Vance at founding and referenced consistently across product decisions, leadership communications, and employee onboarding.

## How it works

**1. "Clarity over cleverness"**
Products and fees should be explainable in one sentence. Applied in practice: no overdraft fees (Best Checking declines transactions instead), full rate transparency in-app (Best Savings), transparent credit terms (Best Credit — "no gotchas").

**2. "Trust is the balance sheet"**
Every decision is weighed against customer trust — the idea that the bank's most valuable asset isn't deposits, it's reputation. Referenced in risk decisions and Eleanor Vance's public-facing communications.

**3. "Small bank feel, big bank reliability"**
Personal service (branch staff, RM relationships, direct-dial support) backed by serious infrastructure (Vault, Conduit, Sentinel). Eleanor Vance cited Conduit specifically as a "big bank reliability" investment that protects the small-bank feel as the bank scales.
