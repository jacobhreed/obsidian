## Summary

14 locations as of 2026: 10 standard branches, 2 support centers (Charlotte and Atlanta), and 1 HQ flagship (South End Charlotte). Network spans NC, GA, TN, SC. Two locations (Atlanta Buckhead and Columbia SC) are under review for consolidation.

## Key points

- South End Charlotte (BR-001) is the HQ and largest branch with 180 staff
- Both support hubs (Charlotte 210 staff, Atlanta 140 staff) are the heaviest-staffed non-HQ locations
- Buckhead and Columbia confirmed for 2026 consolidation per 2026-Q1 newsletter — staff offered roles at nearby branches/hubs
- All branches opened 2011–2021; the most recent is Asheville (2021)
- Network is concentrated in Charlotte (4 locations) and Atlanta (3 locations)
