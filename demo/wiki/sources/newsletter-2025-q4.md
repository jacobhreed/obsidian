## Summary

Year-end 2025 newsletter summarizing a record year: $236M revenue, $47.2M net income, $4.2B deposits, 850K customers. Three defining moments: Conduit GA, Sentinel becoming core, and the cards team forming for Q1 2026. Previews branch consolidation decisions and the 2026 revenue plan shift from deposits to credit.

## Key points

- 2025 was a record year: $236M revenue, $47.2M net income, 20% margin
- ~800,000 deposit accounts across roughly 850,000 active customers
- Three defining moments of 2025: (1) Conduit GA, (2) Sentinel wired into every transaction, (3) Marcus Bell's team built and ready for Q1 2026
- Product scoreboard YE 2025: Checking ~520K accounts/$2.2B; Savings ~280K accounts/$2.0B; Credit launching Q1 2026
- Commercial book: 15 named clients (Meridian Health, Vertex Manufacturing, others); RMs Derek Voss, Amara Osei, Liam Brennan, Sandra Pierce
- Branch update: Buckhead (Atlanta) and Columbia (SC) flagged for possible 2026 consolidation as digital-first trend continues
- Priya Raman quote: "Best Credit changes our revenue mix for the first time in years... the engine that takes us toward $388M by 2028"
