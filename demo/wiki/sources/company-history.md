## Summary

Best Bank, N.A. is a digital-first regional bank founded in Charlotte, NC in 2011 by Eleanor Vance. Originally a single no-fee checking account, it has grown into a $4.2B-deposit bank serving ~850,000 customers with three product lines (Best Checking, Best Savings, Best Credit). The document traces the bank's milestones from founding through 2026 and articulates its culture.

## Key points

- Founded 2011 by Eleanor Vance; $48M Series A; first branch in Charlotte's South End; staff of 19
- Original product: Best Checking (no-fee). Crossed 25,000 accounts within 18 months via referral program
- Deposits hit $1B in 2016, $3B in 2023, $4.2B in 2025
- 2021: Samuel Chen joins as CTO, begins modernizing data stack
- 2022: Compass (CRM) and Lighthouse (BI) launch
- 2023: Goes nationwide digitally; Raj Patel hired to build Data Engineering
- 2025: Conduit data pipeline goes live (Q3), unifying Vault, Compass, Sentinel → Reservoir
- 2026: Best Credit launches (Q1) — first credit product in bank's history
- "Best" name was accidental — from "best-in-class bank" placeholder in pitch decks
- Three culture values: "Clarity over cleverness," "Trust is the balance sheet," "Small bank feel, big bank reliability"
