## Summary

Q1 2026 newsletter announcing Best Credit launch — the bank's first credit product and biggest product event since Best Savings in 2013. Covers go-to-market strategy (existing deposit customers first), in-house underwriting approach, Conduit data work underway for card data domains, and confirmed branch consolidations at Buckhead and Columbia.

## Key points

- Best Credit Card (no annual fee, 2% back) and Best Line of Credit launched Q1 2026
- Lisa Tran (CPO) quote: "conservative limits, transparent terms, no gotchas" — consistent with culture values
- Go-to-market: existing deposit customers first (lowest cost, best risk profile), led by James Whitfield
- Underwriting: Nadia Fields using Sentinel signals + Reservoir deposit history
- Fraud-ready from day one: every card swipe scored in real time by Sentinel; Card Ops handles disputes from Atlanta
- Conduit roadmap: new card data domains (transactions, balances, disputes) landing 2026 H2 — Raj Patel + Marcus Bell partnership
- Lighthouse dashboards for adoption and loss rates being built by Data Eng
- 2026 targets: ~12% checking-customer adoption, $310M balances YE, credit losses ~2.8%, fraud <10bps
- Buckhead and Columbia branch consolidations confirmed; staff offered roles at nearby branches/hubs
- Eleanor Vance founder quote: "would I be happy to use it myself?" — the test every product must pass
