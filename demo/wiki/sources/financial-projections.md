## Summary

FP&A document (Grace Kim / Priya Raman) projecting revenue from $236M (2025 actual) to $388M (2028) — ~18% CAGR — anchored on deposit growth, Best Credit launch, and operating leverage from the data stack. Net margin is expected to expand from 20% (2025) to 23% (2028).

## Key points

- **2025 actuals:** $236M revenue, $47.2M net income, 20% margin, $4.2B deposits, 850K customers
- **2028 targets:** $388M revenue, $89.2M net income, 23% margin, $6.5B deposits, 1.27M customers
- Three growth drivers: (1) deposit growth, (2) Best Credit launch, (3) operating leverage from Conduit/Lighthouse/Sentinel
- **Net interest income** is the largest stream: $172M (2025) → $262M (2028), driven by deposit growth + Best Credit revolving balances
- **Card interchange** is the fastest-growing: $28M → $62M, fueled entirely by Best Credit
- Best Credit balances projected: $310M (2026) → $980M (2028), assuming 12% checking-customer adoption within 18 months
- Credit losses modeled at 2.8% of balances; fraud losses target <10bps card volume by 2027 (down from 18bps)
- Efficiency ratio: 62% (2025) → 56% (2028), driven by Conduit automation
- NIM: 3.85% (2025) → 4.10% (2028); sensitive to rate environment (100bps drop = ~$9M NII hit in 2027)
- 2026 investment priorities: Best Credit go-to-market (Whitfield), Conduit hardening, 2 branch consolidations
