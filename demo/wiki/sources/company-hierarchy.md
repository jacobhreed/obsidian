## Summary

Org chart as of June 2026. Eight C-suite executives report to Eleanor Vance. Engineering (Samuel Chen) is the largest technical org. Product (Lisa Tran) owns the three product lines. Risk & Compliance are split between CRO Maria Delgado and CCO Angela Brooks. Operations (David Okafor) owns the branch network and customer support.

## Key points

- **ELT (8 direct reports to CEO):** Okafor (COO), Raman (CFO), Chen (CTO), Delgado (CRO), Brooks (CCO), Whitfield (CMO), Tran (CPO)
- **Engineering sub-teams:** Platform (Kevin Mbeki) — Vault; Data Eng (Raj Patel) — Conduit/Reservoir/Lighthouse; Mobile & Web (Sofia Ramos); Security (Daniel Cho)
- **Product sub-teams:** Deposits (Hannah Lee) — Checking + Savings; Lending & Cards (Marcus Bell) — Best Credit; Platform & Data Products (Olivia Grant) — Compass
- **Risk:** Fraud Ops (Tomás Herrera, Sentinel alerts); Credit Risk (Nadia Fields, underwriting); BSA/AML (Robert Ellis)
- **Finance:** FP&A (Grace Kim); Treasury; Accounting
- **Operations:** Branch Network, Customer Support, Card Operations (Atlanta)
- Total: ~1,240 employees across 10 departments
