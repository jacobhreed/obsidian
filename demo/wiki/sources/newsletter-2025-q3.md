## Summary

Internal quarterly newsletter (Q3 2025) focused on two events: Conduit going GA and Sentinel moving from pilot to core consumer. Monthly revenue was running at $20M/mo (up from $18.2M in Q1), on track for the $4.2B deposit year-end target. Teases a "major product launch in early 2026" (Best Credit).

## Key points

- Conduit went GA this quarter — unifies Vault, Compass, Sentinel data into Reservoir for the first time
- Raj Patel quote: "Before Conduit, three teams could pull the same number and get three answers. Now we have one source of truth."
- Downstream benefits: Finance gets daily reconciled data; Risk gets near-real-time Sentinel signals; dashboards now agree
- Sentinel moved from pilot to core Conduit consumer; fraud alerts now stream in <2 minutes
- Monthly revenue at ~$20M in August, up from ~$18.2M in January
- Marcus Bell's Lending & Cards team staffing up ahead of 2026 product launch (unnamed, but Best Credit)
- Card Operations standing up in Atlanta hub
- Eleanor Vance quote framing Conduit as "small bank feel, big bank reliability" investment
