## Summary

Product documentation for Best Checking, the bank's flagship transaction account launched at founding in 2011. Three tiers: base (always free), Plus ($12/mo, waivable), Business ($15/mo, waivable). As of YE 2025: ~520,000 accounts, $2.2B in balances. Primary acquisition product and cross-sell entry point.

## Key points

- **Base tier:** $0 always. No minimum balance. No overdraft fee (transaction declined instead)
- **Best Checking+:** $12/mo waived at $1,500 avg balance or $2,000 monthly deposit. Adds ATM rebates and early direct deposit (up to 2 days)
- **Best Business Checking:** $15/mo waived at $5,000 avg balance
- No overdraft trap is a direct expression of "Clarity over cleverness" culture value
- "Bring a Friend" referral program is a descendant of the original 2011 launch offer — still the #1 acquisition channel
- Tech stack: Vault (ledger) → Conduit → Reservoir → Lighthouse (analytics); Sentinel (fraud); Compass (customer 360)
- 12% of checking customers expected to adopt Best Credit within 18 months (per financial plan)
- Wire fees: $15 domestic, $35 international; paper statements $3/mo
