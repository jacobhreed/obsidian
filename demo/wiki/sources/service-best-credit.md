## Summary

Product documentation for Best Credit, the bank's first credit line, launched Q1 2026. Two products: Best Credit Card (no annual fee, 2% cash back, 0% intro APR 12mo) and Best Line of Credit ($2K–$50K, 9.99%–22.99% APR). Underwritten in-house using Sentinel signals + deposit history. Primary growth lever for 2026–2028 revenue plan.

## Key points

- **Best Credit Card:** $0 annual fee; 2% cash back everywhere, 3% on top category; 0% for 12mo then 18.99%–26.99% variable; $500–$25K limits
- **Best Line of Credit:** $2K–$50K; 9.99%–22.99% APR; revolving
- In-house underwriting by Nadia Fields (Credit Risk) using Sentinel + Reservoir deposit history
- "Known customer" lift: existing checking/savings customers get higher approval odds (bank already sees cash flow)
- Credit losses modeled at 2.8% of balances; fraud target <10bps card volume by 2027
- Go-to-market: existing deposit customers first (lowest cost, best risk profile). Led by James Whitfield (CMO)
- Card Operations (COO, Atlanta) handles disputes/chargebacks; Fraud Ops (Tomás Herrera) handles Sentinel alerts
- Vault holds card subledger; Conduit card data domains coming 2026 H2; Lighthouse to track adoption and loss rates
- Plan: ~12% of checking customers adopt within 18 months; $310M balances by YE 2026, $980M by 2028
