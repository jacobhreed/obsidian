## Overview

Best Checking is the bank's original and flagship product, launched in 2011. It is the primary acquisition vehicle and the top of the cross-sell funnel. Three tiers serve consumers (Base, Plus) and small businesses (Business Checking).

## Key facts

- **Launched:** 2011 (at founding)
- **PM:** Hannah Lee, under CPO Lisa Tran
- **YE 2025:** ~520,000 accounts; $2.2B in balances
  - Base tier: 430K accounts, $1.05B, avg $2,442
  - Plus tier: 78K accounts, $620M, avg $7,949
  - Business tier: 12K accounts, $530M, avg $44,167
- **No overdraft fee** — declined instead; direct expression of "Clarity over cleverness"
- **"Bring a Friend" referral program** — the bank's #1 acquisition channel; descends from the original 2011 referral offer
- Cross-sell: 12% of checking customers expected to adopt Best Credit within 18 months
- Card interchange currently comes from debit transactions on this product; Best Credit card will add a new interchange stream
