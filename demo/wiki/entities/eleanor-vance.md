## Overview

Founder and CEO of Best Bank. Former community-bank operations executive who founded the bank in 2011 with a $48M Series A and a conviction that mid-size regional banks were losing customers on experience, not price.

## Role in this wiki

The central figure in Best Bank's history and strategy. Her founding philosophy — "Banking at its Best" and the three culture values — runs through every major product and infrastructure decision.

## Key facts

- Founded Best Bank in Charlotte, NC in October 2011 with a staff of 19
- Raised $48M Series A; obtained a national bank charter
- Coined the three culture principles: "Clarity over cleverness," "Trust is the balance sheet," "Small bank feel, big bank reliability"
- "Best" name was an accident — from "best-in-class bank" placeholder in her pitch deck
- Consistent presence in internal comms (The Best Brief) with quotes anchoring every major milestone
- 2026 Q1 credit launch quote: "would I be happy to use it myself?" — her test for every product
