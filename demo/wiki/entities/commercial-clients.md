## Overview

Best Bank's commercial banking book as of YE 2025: 15 named clients across 8 industries, managed by 4 relationship managers. Total visible deposit balances: ~$69.3M. All clients use Best Business Checking; several also carry savings and credit products.

## Key facts

- **4 RMs:** Derek Voss, Amara Osei, Liam Brennan, Sandra Pierce
- **15 clients** across healthcare, manufacturing, real estate, transportation, food & beverage, tech, construction, publishing, retail
- Largest depositor: **Meridian Health Group** ($12.8M, healthcare, Liam Brennan)
- Longest-tenure client: **Vertex Manufacturing** (since 2015, $11.2M, Derek Voss)
- Already using Best Credit Card (launched Q1 2026): Meridian Health Group, Summit Outdoor Gear
- Using Best Line of Credit: Sterling Logistics, Harborview Restaurants, Ironwood Construction, Greenline Freight
- Combined visible annual revenues of clients range from $9.5M (Cascade Coffee) to $210M (Meridian Health Group)
