## Overview

Best Bank operates 14 locations across NC, GA, TN, and SC as of 2026: 10 standard branches, 2 support hubs, and 1 HQ flagship. Two branches (Buckhead and Columbia) are being consolidated in 2026 as digital-first customer behavior reduces foot traffic.

## Key facts

- **Owner:** David Okafor (COO), Operations & Branch Network department (~280 staff in branches)
- **Total locations:** 14 (10 branches + 2 support hubs + 1 HQ flagship)
- **HQ:** South End Charlotte (BR-001), opened 2011, 180 staff
- **Support hubs:** Charlotte (BR-013, 210 staff) and Atlanta (BR-014, 140 staff, opened 2020)
- **2026 consolidations:** Atlanta Buckhead (BR-007, 14 staff) and Columbia SC (BR-011, 11 staff); staff offered roles at nearby locations
- Geographic concentration: Charlotte (4 locations), Atlanta (3 locations), Carolinas (5 locations)
- 92% of new accounts were opened online by end of 2020 (pandemic accelerant)
