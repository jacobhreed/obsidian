## Overview

Best Savings, launched 2013, is the bank's interest-bearing product line comprising High-Yield Savings accounts and CDs. It is the largest single contributor to net interest income and a key deposit stability lever.

## Key facts

- **Launched:** 2013
- **PM:** Hannah Lee, under CPO Lisa Tran
- **YE 2025:** ~280,000 accounts; $2.0B in balances
  - High-Yield Savings: 240K accounts, $1.35B, avg $5,625, APY ~3.60%
  - CDs: 40K accounts, $650M, avg $16,250, APY ~4.20%
- **Notable features:** Goal Buckets (2024, top-requested), Round-ups from Best Checking, full rate transparency in-app
- **Rate setting:** Treasury (Priya Raman) sets rates; tiered to manage NIM risk
- Rate risk: if customers shift from low-cost checking into high-APY savings, it compresses NIM. A 100bps rate cut would reduce 2027 NII by ~$9M
- NII from Best Savings ($2B balance base) is the bank's largest single revenue driver
