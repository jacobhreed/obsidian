## Overview

Best Credit is the bank's first credit product line, launched Q1 2026 — the largest product event since Best Savings in 2013. It comprises the Best Credit Card (no-fee, 2% cash back) and the Best Line of Credit ($2K–$50K revolving). It is the primary growth lever in the 2026–2028 financial plan.

## Key facts

- **Launched:** Q1 2026
- **PM:** Marcus Bell, under CPO Lisa Tran
- **Risk owner:** Nadia Fields (Credit Risk), under CRO Maria Delgado
- In-house underwriting using Sentinel signals + Reservoir deposit history; "known customer" approval lift for existing deposit holders
- **Credit Card:** $0 annual fee; 2% cash back, 3% on top spend category; 0% intro APR 12mo then 18.99–26.99% variable; $500–$25K limits
- **Line of Credit:** $2K–$50K; 9.99–22.99% APR; revolving
- **2026 targets:** 12% of checking customers adopt within 18 months; $310M balances by YE; credit losses 2.8%; fraud <10bps
- **2028 targets:** $980M balances; card interchange reaches $62M/yr
- Card Operations (Atlanta, 23 staff) handles disputes/chargebacks
- Conduit card data domains (transactions, balances, disputes) landing H2 2026
