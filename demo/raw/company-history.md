# Best Bank — Company History

> *"We started with a simple promise: banking shouldn't feel like a chore."*
> — Eleanor Vance, Founder & CEO

## Overview

**Best Bank, N.A.** is a digital-first regional bank headquartered in Charlotte,
North Carolina. Founded in 2011, the bank serves consumers and small-to-midsize
businesses across the Southeast and, since 2023, nationwide through its mobile and
web platforms. As of year-end 2025, Best Bank held approximately **$4.2 billion in
total deposits**, served roughly **850,000 active customers**, and employed about
**1,240 people**.

Best Bank offers three core product lines: [Best Checking](./service-best-checking.md),
[Best Savings](./service-best-savings.md), and [Best Credit](./service-best-credit.md).

## Founding (2011)

Best Bank was founded by **Eleanor Vance**, a former community-bank operations
executive, who believed mid-size regional banks were losing customers to megabanks
not on price but on experience. She raised a $48M Series A and obtained a national
bank charter, opening the first branch in Charlotte's South End in October 2011 with
a staff of 19.

The original product was a single no-fee checking account. Within 18 months the bank
had crossed 25,000 accounts, largely through word of mouth and a referral program that
still echoes in today's "Bring a Friend" promotions.

## Why "Best"?

The name was almost an accident. In early pitch decks Vance used "Best-in-class bank"
as a placeholder. Investors kept referring to it as "the Best bank," and the name
stuck. The tagline **"Banking at its Best"** followed naturally and has been unchanged
since 2011.

## Culture & values

Best Bank organizes its culture around three principles, referenced throughout
onboarding and in [*The Best Brief*](./newsletter-2025-q4.md) newsletters:

1. **Clarity over cleverness** — products and fees should be understandable in one sentence.
2. **Trust is the balance sheet** — every decision is weighed against customer trust.
3. **Small bank feel, big bank reliability** — personal service backed by serious infrastructure.
