## 🚀 Headline: Conduit is live

After eight months of build, our new data pipeline — **[Conduit](./data-pipeline-conduit.md)** —
went into general availability this quarter. For the first time, data from **Vault**,
**Compass**, and **Sentinel** flows through a single governed pipeline into
**Reservoir**, powering every **Lighthouse** dashboard.

> "Before Conduit, three teams could pull the same number and get three answers.
> Now we have one source of truth." — **Raj Patel**, Head of Data

What this means for you:

- **Finance** gets reconciled revenue data daily (no more month-end scramble).
- **Risk** gets near-real-time fraud signals into Sentinel.
- **Everyone** gets dashboards that agree with each other.

Huge thanks to the Data Engineering team and to Platform Engineering (Kevin Mbeki's
group) for the Vault change-data-capture work.

The **Sentinel** fraud engine moved from pilot to a core Conduit consumer this quarter.
