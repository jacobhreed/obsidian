# The Best Brief — 2025 Q4 · Year in Review

> *Best Bank's internal newsletter. Published quarterly by Marketing & Comms (CMO James Whitfield).*

---

## 🎉 A record year

We closed 2025 with **$236.0M in revenue** and an estimated **$47.2M in net income** —
our best year ever, and a 20% net margin. Deposits reached **$4.2B** across roughly
**800,000 deposit accounts** and **~850,000 active customers**.

Full breakdown lives in the [Financial Projections](./financial-projections.md) deck;
the monthly detail is in [`monthly_revenue_2025.csv`](./data/monthly_revenue_2025.csv).

---

## 🏆 The year in three moments

1. **Conduit went GA (Q3).** Our [data pipeline](./data-pipeline-conduit.md) unified the
   stack and ended the era of conflicting reports.
2. **Sentinel became core.** Real-time fraud scoring is now wired into every transaction.
3. **We built the cards team.** Marcus Bell's Lending & Cards group is ready for the
   Q1 2026 launch of [Best Credit](./service-best-credit.md). 🪪
